The Challenge SMEs Face

Millions of small retailers in Africa still operate manually—tracking orders on paper, relying on phone calls, and lacking proper records. This prevents them from accessing financing, managing stock efficiently, and scaling their business. Many lose revenue, experience supply gaps, and remain invisible to financiers and investors.

Our Solution

Bomawave provides a lightweight digital platform that automatically captures transactions, tracks inventory, and builds a credible financial footprint for SMEs. Retailers gain visibility into their operations, wholesalers receive accurate demand data, and financiers can assess creditworthiness with reliable real-time information.

Our expertise in finance, credit management, risk analysis, and the business environment ensures that we design solutions that are practical, scalable, and directly relevant to the African SME ecosystem.

Who We Build For

We focus on informal retailers and small business owners who struggle with manual operations. By digitizing their sales, orders, and inventory, we unlock growth potential, provide access to smart financing, and make their businesses sustainable and investment-ready.
